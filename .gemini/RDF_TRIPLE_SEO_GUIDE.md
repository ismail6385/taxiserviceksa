## Key SEO Elements to Include

### 1. **Page Title (Meta Title)**
Format: `[Service] in [Location] | [Primary Benefit] & [Secondary Benefit]`
- Keep under 60 characters
- Include location + service + benefit
- Use RDF structure

### 2. **H1 Heading**
Format: `[Service] in [Location] Helping [Target Audience] with [Specific Services]`
- Clear Subject â†’ Predicate â†’ Object
- Natural language
- Include location and intent

### 3. **Meta Description**
Format: `Professional [service] in [location] helping [audience] with [service 1], [service 2], [service 3], and [service 4].`
- 150-160 characters
- Include multiple services (objects)
- Add availability (24/7)
- Natural, benefit-focused

### 4. **Keywords**
Focus on:
- Location + Service (Makkah taxi)
- Location + Specific Service (Umrah taxi Makkah)
- Route-based (Makkah to Madinah taxi)
- Intent-based (Ziyarat taxi, Airport transfer)
