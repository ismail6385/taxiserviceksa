## ğŸ¨ RDF Triple Content Formula

### For Every Service Page:

#### 1. Title Tag
```
[Service] [Location] | [Benefit] [Action] [Target Audience]

Example:
Airport Transfer Jeddah | 24/7 Chauffeur Service from King Abdulaziz Airport
```

#### 2. H1 Heading
```
[Adjective] [Service] in [Location] - [Benefit] [Target Audience]

Example:
Professional Airport Transfer in Jeddah - Reliable Chauffeur Service
```

#### 3. Opening Paragraph (First 100 words)
```
As a [trusted/premier/leading] [service] in [location], we specialize in 
[specific service] for [target audience]. Our [service type] helps [audience] 
with [specific benefits].

Example:
As a trusted taxi service in Jeddah, we specialize in providing 24/7 airport 
transfers from King Abdulaziz International Airport. Our professional chauffeurs 
help travelers with luxury pickups, meet and greet services, and comfortable 
rides to hotels, Makkah, and Madinah.
```
