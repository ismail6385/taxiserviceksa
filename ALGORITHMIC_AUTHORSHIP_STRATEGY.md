# Algorithmic Authorship & Entity Strategy (Blue Hat SEO)

Based on "5x Organic Growth with Semantics" (YouTube Video 7), this document outlines the **"Algorithmic Authorship"** strategy to prove human effort and expertise to Google.

## 1. Ranking Pattern Analysis
- **The "Dip" Signal:** After the initial ranking increase, a slight drop or balancing is normal.
- **The Bounce Back:** A quick drop often precedes a massive ranking increase, especially if aligned with core updates.
- **Action:** Do not panic during volatility. Interpret it as an algorithmic test of your "Information Graph."

## 2. Structured Authorship (The "Linguist" Pattern)
- **Consistency is Key:** Define authors with a *consistent, topic-relevant title*.
    - *Example:* Instead of just "Writer," use "**Transport Logistics Specialist**" or "**Umrah Route Expert**."
    - This creates a repetitive signal that this entity (Author) belongs to this topic (Transport).
- **Third-Party Validation:**
    - The author should exist on third-party sites (LinkedIn, ResearchGate, Quora, Medium) with the *same face, name, and title*.
    - This triangulation proves the "Real World Entity" status.
    - **Google's Motto:** "People write to people for people." Proving human effort is a ranking factor.

## 3. The "Entity Graph" Loop
1.  **Define Expert:** Create a clean bio for an author (e.g., "Abdulrahman - Senior Fleet Manager").
2.  **External Link:** If possible, create a personal site or strong social profile for this expert.
3.  **Reciprocal Link:** Link from the Author's external profile *back* to the specific articles they wrote on TaxiServiceKSA.
4.  **Result:** This increases the "Confidence Score" of the relationship between the Expert and the Content.
