## 💡 KORAY'S METHODOLOGY - KEY LEARNINGS

### **1. Entity-First Always**
> "Tom Hanks is the entity... according to the predicates... search engine will understand"

**Applied:**
- "GMC Yukon accommodates..." (not "Our GMC Yukon can...")
- "VIP Transfer KSA operates..." (not "We operate...")
- "Airport transfers in Saudi Arabia..." (not "We provide airport transfers...")

### **2. Micro-Semantics Matter**
> "By just changing the order of words... you can create a macro difference"

**Applied:**
- "Taxi Service in Saudi Arabia" (entity first)
- "GMC Yukon taxi service includes driver" (entity → predicate → object)
- "Service operates 24/7" (service entity, not "we")

### **3. Cost of Retrieval**
> "Decreasing the cost that you are creating for the search engine"

**Applied:**
- Factual statements (easier to verify)
- Clear entities (easier to parse)
- No ambiguity (no "reliable", "best")
- Specific details (7 passengers, 5 bags, not "spacious")
