# Jeddah — Spot-Level Complete Topical Map

## ✈️ PRIMARY AUDIENCE: International Travelers
> **Core Insight:** Humari service mainly international tourists, Umrah/Hajj pilgrims, cruise passengers, aur business travelers ke liye hai — locals ke liye nahi. Isliye har page ki language, content, aur keywords **English-first** honi chahiye aur international user ki problems solve karni chahiye.

### International Traveler Personas (In Priority Order)
| Persona | Where They Come From | Key Pain Points | Our Solution |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| 🕌 Umrah/Hajj Pilgrim | UK, USA, Malaysia, Indonesia, Pakistan | Confusing transport from Jeddah to Makkah | Pre-booked private transfer, Muslim driver |

### Page Content Requirements for International Audience
Every page MUST include:
- ✅ **Pricing in SAR + USD reference** (e.g., "SAR 180 (~$48 USD)")
- ✅ **English-speaking driver mention** prominently
- ✅ **Safety & trust signals** (years in operation, fleet photos)
- ✅ **WhatsApp booking** (international travelers prefer WhatsApp over phone calls)
- ✅ **Muslim driver badge** for pilgrimage routes
- ✅ **Payment methods** (cash, card, transfer)
- ✅ **"Meet & Greet" language** — not just "pickup"
- ✅ **Flight delay policy** (wait time included)
- ✅ **No hidden charges** stated explicitly
- ✅ **Cancellation policy** in English

## IMPLEMENTATION STRATEGY

### Step 1 — Common Page Template (1 baar banao)
Ek master `TransferPageTemplate` React component banao jisme sirf yeh values dynamic hon:
- `spotName` (e.g., "Ritz-Carlton Jeddah")
- `address` (e.g., "Al Hamra District, Corniche Road")
- `distance` (e.g., "28 km from KAIA Airport")
- `duration` (e.g., "35 minutes")
- `fromPrice` (e.g., "SAR 100")
- `faqs` (array — 3-4 spot-specific questions)
- `nearbySpots` (links to 2-3 neighboring spots for internal linking)

### Step 2 — Page Generation (1 route file = 1 page)
Each page in `app/(main)/transfers/[spot]/page.tsx` with:
- Unique `metadata` title + description
- `Service` JSON-LD schema
- `FAQPage` JSON-LD schema (auto-extracted)
- WhatsApp CTA pointing to `+966569487569`
- Internal links to related spots

### Step 3 — Internal Linking Hub
Ek `/transfers/jeddah/` hub page banao jo **all spots** list kare. Yeh page Google ko crawl signal deta hai aur sitelinks mein help karta hai.

### Step 4 — Sitemap Priority
Har transfers page ko `priority: 0.8` pe sitemap mein add karo.
